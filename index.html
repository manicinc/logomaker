Project Genesis & AI Collaboration

  • This Logomaker is an experimental project born from Manic Agency's exploration into Human+AI collaborative development.
  • A significant portion (~90%) of the underlying code structure, boilerplate, and even specific functions were generated or assisted by Large Language Models under human direction and refinement.
  • Key AI collaborators included models from the **GPT-4o**, **Claude 3 (Opus/Sonnet)**, and **Gemini Pro** families (as of early 2025 - models evolve rapidly!).
  • It's a testament to rapid prototyping possibilities, but also highlights the need for careful human oversight, debugging (as you've seen!), and architectural design.
  • Consider this a fascinating artifact of modern development workflows!

On SVG Animations

  • Yes! Active CSS animations (Pulse, Float, etc.) are embedded directly into exported SVGs using `style` tags and `@keyframes`.
  • View the SVG directly in modern browsers that support CSS within SVG, and watch your logo come alive!
  • (Note: Compatibility can vary depending on the SVG viewer or specific browser version.)

Project Future & Roadmap

  • Logomaker began as an internal Manic tool, released alongside PortaPack.
  • It remains free, open-source (MIT License), and hosted via GitHub Pages. While major new features aren't planned for this specific version, community contributions (PRs) are welcome!
  • Manic Agency continues R&D for more advanced AI-powered creative generation platforms. Follow progress at Manic.agency!

Forge Your Mark with Logomaker üé®

Logomaker is a free, open-source creative tool by Manic Agency, designed for crafting striking text-based logos with ease and portability.

Core Magic: Dive into extensive font support (with BYOF & embedding!), sculpt text with styling, gradients, borders, and electrifying effects. Bring logos to life with animations, set the stage with customizable backgrounds, and export your creations as pixel-perfect PNGs, scalable SVGs, or animation frame sequences (ZIP). Share your designs instantly with unique URLs.

Genesis Note: This tool is an experimental venture into Human+AI collaboration by Manic Agency, with models like GPT-4o, Claude 3, and Gemini Pro assisting in ~90% of the code generation under human guidance. (More Info)

Source & Freedom: GitHub (MIT License) - Use it, fork it, enhance it!
